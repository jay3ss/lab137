Converting a PowerPoint PPT to a PDF File

Published Fri 01 May 2020 in articles

by Jay Ess ppt powerpoint til pdf convert

I'm currently taking an online class and the professor gives out PowerPoint slides as part of his lecture. I don't like the LibreOffice version of PowerPoint (Impress) and prefer to use PDF files. So, I found a simple one-liner from Ask Ubuntu that's worked well so far.

libreoffice --headless --invisible --convert-to pdf *.ppt
